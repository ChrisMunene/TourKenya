Masada is the name for a site of ancient palaces and fortifications in Southern District of Israel, on top of an isolated rock plateau, or horst, on the eastern edge of the Judaean Desert, overlooking the Dead Sea. Situated on the Naivasha South road, 500 meters off the Mai Mahiu - Naivasha Highway, Masada Hotel is the getaway destination you have been waiting for.. as the name suggests, Masada, a fortress - a place of comfort complete, with ultra modern facilities.

Villa Rosa Kempinski


Offering the perfect fusion of European luxury and Kenyan hospitality, Villa Rosa Kempinski is positioned between the city and countryside. The hotel is a unique destination where guests can spend time relaxing or working, and in addition to the 200 rooms and suites distributed throughout its ten floors, you will also find exquisite dining opportunities here. In addition to our all-day dining restaurant, Cafe Villa Rosa, you can try a range of menus and dining styles across K Lounge; Balcony Bar; Pan-Asian 88 Lounge and Restaurant; Italian Restaurant LUCCA; and our Levant -style lounge and restaurant Tambourin.
